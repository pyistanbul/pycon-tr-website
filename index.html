Join us at PyCon Turkey 2020 in Istanbul!

This will be our first international PyCon conference where we aim to bring Pythonistas of all levels around the world together for exciting talks for two days. PyCon Turkey is a nonprofit event facilitated by Istanbul Python Users Group (PyIstanbul), an organization focusing to support and educate Python users in Turkey.

We will announce the details soon.
Make sure to tune in for announcements!

The Venue

The conference will be held at Albert Long Hall, at Bogazici University. The hall has perfect acoustic characteristics with a capacity of 400 seats.

Bogazici University is a major research university located at the heart of Bosphorus. It has a vibrant campus welcoming dynamic minds from all over the world.
