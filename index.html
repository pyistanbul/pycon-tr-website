Accessibility

We want to remove all the barriers to join the meeting. So:

  • It's possible to enter the venue with a wheelchair. You can use elevators and there are accessible toilets.
  • The university has disabled parking facilities.
  • We have retreat spots for people with sensory issues.
  • At the venue, you can always contact to an attendant in any case. We will help you.
